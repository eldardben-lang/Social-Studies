Overview
Canada's 6 Landform Regions
Each region has its own unique landscape, climate and natural resources
1. Canadian Shield
Ancient rock covering nearly half of Canada
2. Interior Plains
Flat grasslands across the prairie provinces
3. Western Cordillera
Rocky mountains along the west coast
4. Arctic Lands
Frozen tundra and islands in the far north
5. Hudson Bay Lowlands
Flat swampy land around Hudson Bay
6. Appalachian Region
Ancient rounded mountains in eastern Canada
🗺️
Key fact!The Canadian Shield covers nearly 50% of Canada's total land — the ancient rocky heart of the country, over 1 billion years old!
